Notch first started the project of minecraft as a small game know as 'Cave Game'.Then later, before he released it in 2009, he renamed it 'minecraft'.
At the first minecon held, 50 people attened.
The game was very advanced for its time. Even today it has some features that most other sandbox games don't have like commonds and custom structures.
The developers are very fan-friendly which is shown is the '.minecraft' folder where mojang made a folder specifically for addons made by the fan base.We cannot se this in most other games.
We can also see the friendliness with free minecraft.
The game is quite pricey so a company has made a launcher called, Tlauncher.
Minecraft could copyright strike this, but it understood the fact that the game was expensive and even lets tlauncher link to the real minecraft.
